Privacy Policy for BetBlocked

Last Updated: September 18, 2025

1. Introduction

BetBlocked ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application designed to support gambling addiction recovery.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Journal Entries: Personal reflections and recovery progress notes
  • Progress Data: Streak counters, completed exercises, and milestones
  • Settings: App preferences and configuration choices
  • AI Interactions: Messages sent to our AI support assistants

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Analytics: App feature usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Device Information: iOS version, device model (for compatibility)
  • Crash Reports: Technical data to improve app stability

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Recovery Support: Personalize your recovery journey and track progress
  • AI Assistance: Provide contextual support through our AI assistants
  • App Improvement: Enhance features and fix technical issues
  • Safety Features: Implement blocking and restriction capabilities

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Most personal data is stored locally on your device
  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • No Selling: We never sell your personal information to third parties
  • Minimal Cloud Data: Only essential sync data is stored in the cloud

5. Third-Party Services

  • AI Services: Anonymous conversations with AI assistants (no personal identification)
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage data to improve the app experience
  • VPN Services: Premium blocking features (subscription required)

6. Your Rights

  • Access: View all data we have about you
  • Deletion: Request complete removal of your data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Correction: Update or correct your information

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for recovery support. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the app settings.

8. Children's Privacy

BetBlocked is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

9. State-Specific Rights (Florida)

As a Florida-based company, we comply with Florida privacy laws. Florida residents have additional rights under state law, including the right to request information about data sharing with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not share personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through the app with at least 30 days' notice.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights:

  • Email: betblockedceo@gmail.com
  • Support: Available through the app's Settings > Contact Support
  • Address: 3822 Woodfield Ct, Coconut Creek, FL 33073
  • Response Time: We respond to privacy requests within 30 days

This privacy policy complies with GDPR, CCPA, and Apple App Store requirements for health and wellness applications.
